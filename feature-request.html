Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a feature request? +

Use the form above to provide details about the feature you'd like to see. Be as specific as possible to help us understand your request.

Will I be notified when my feature is implemented? +

While we can't promise individual notifications for every request, we'll do our best to keep the community updated on new features through our blog and newsletter.

Can I suggest features for specific tools? +

Yes, please use the "Related Tool" dropdown in the form to specify which tool your feature request is for. If it's a general feature, select "General."

How are feature requests prioritized? +

We prioritize feature requests based on factors such as user demand, feasibility, and alignment with our product roadmap. Your input helps us make these decisions.
