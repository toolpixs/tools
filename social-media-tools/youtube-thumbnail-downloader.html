What is a YouTube Thumbnail?

A YouTube thumbnail is a small preview image that represents your video in YouTube's search results, recommendations, and playlists. It's the first visual element viewers notice, making it critical for attracting clicks and engagement. Thumbnails can be automatically generated by YouTube or customized by creators to better reflect the video's content.
