Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

ToolPix, operated by Time Load Company, uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site usage, and provide personalized content. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, improve functionality, and provide insights into how users interact with the site.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure our website functions properly (e.g., navigation, tool access).
  • Remember your preferences, such as dark mode settings.
  • Analyze site performance and user behavior to improve our services.
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function (e.g., session management). These cannot be disabled.
  • Preference Cookies: Store your settings, like language or theme preferences.
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect anonymous data on site usage (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for personalized ads (if applicable).

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect site functionality.
  • Our Cookie Banner: Adjust preferences via our cookie consent tool (if implemented).
  • Opt-Out Tools: Use tools like Google Analytics Opt-Out.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services, such as:

  • Google Analytics for site usage data.
  • Social media platforms for sharing features.

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date. Please check back regularly.
