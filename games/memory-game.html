How to Play Memory Game: A Beginner's Guide

Memory Game is a fun and challenging card matching game where you flip cards to find matching pairs. Here's how to play:

Objective

Find all matching pairs of cards on a 4x4 grid by flipping two cards at a time.

Controls

  • Click or tap on a card to flip it and reveal its symbol.

Game Rules

  1. Flipping Cards: Click on any card to flip it and see its symbol. You can flip two cards at a time.
  2. Matching Pairs: If the two flipped cards have the same symbol, they stay face-up and are considered matched.
  3. Mismatch: If the two flipped cards do not match, they flip back face-down after a short delay.
  4. Winning: The game is won when all pairs are matched and all cards are face-up.
  5. Scoring: Your score is based on the number of moves (flips) taken. Fewer moves mean a higher score.
