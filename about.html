About ToolPix

ToolPix, powered by Time Load, is a leading platform offering over 200 free online tools to simplify your digital tasks. From YouTube thumbnail downloaders and image converters to PDF editors, AI utilities, code compilers, and engaging games, we provide fast, secure, and user-friendly solutions without requiring sign-ups.

Based in Kannur, Kerala, India, our mission is to empower creators, developers, students, and gamers worldwide with accessible, privacy-focused tools that run entirely in your browser.
