About Toolpix

A solo project empowering digital creativity

About Toolpix

Toolpix, launched on June 10, 2025, is an independent solo project developed under the Time Load brand. It’s a fast-growing platform offering a wide collection of free, privacy-friendly online tools — designed to help users convert, compress, generate, and run digital content effortlessly.

What We Do

Toolpix empowers users with over 50+ web-based tools including:

  • Image Tools: Compress, resize, convert formats (JPG, PNG, etc.)
  • PDF Tools: Merge, split, convert to/from Word
  • AI Tools: Generate images, enhance content (with more coming)
  • Code Tools: Online compilers and formatters

All tools are:

  • Login-free
  • Data-private (100% client-side)
  • Fast, simple, and free

Built by One, For Everyone

Toolpix is created and maintained by Muhammed Ajmal U K, an independent developer from Kannur, Kerala, India. With no team or corporate backing, this project reflects a passion for open access, tech innovation, and community-driven problem solving.

Future Vision

In the near future, Toolpix will introduce:

  • AI Agents for workflow automation
  • All-in-One AI Toolkits
  • Additional utilities for video, audio, and advanced file processing
  • Optional ads to support further development
