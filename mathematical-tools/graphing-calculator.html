About Our Graphing Calculator

Our online graphing calculator allows you to visualize mathematical functions by plotting them on a graph. It supports a variety of functions, including polynomials, trigonometric functions, and exponentials, making it ideal for students, educators, and professionals who need to analyze equations visually.

How to Use the Graphing Calculator

Using our graphing calculator is simple and intuitive:

  • Enter a mathematical function in the input field (e.g., x^2, sin(x), 2*x + 1).
  • Use standard JavaScript math syntax: ^ for exponents, * for multiplication, Math.sin(x) for sine, etc.
  • Click the "Plot Function" button to generate the graph.
  • Use the zoom in (+) and zoom out (-) buttons to adjust the view.
  • The graph displays the function over a default range of x-values (-10 to 10).
  • Supported functions include: sin, cos, tan, log, exp, sqrt, and more.
