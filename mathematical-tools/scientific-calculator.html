Scientific Calculator

About Our Scientific Calculator

Our online scientific calculator is designed for advanced mathematical computations, offering functions like trigonometry (sin, cos, tan), logarithms, exponentials, and factorials. Ideal for students, engineers, and scientists, this tool is accessible without registration and provides instant, accurate results.

How to Use the Scientific Calculator

Using our scientific calculator is straightforward and efficient:

  • Click number buttons to input digits or use constants like π and e.
  • Use operator buttons (+, -, ×, /, ^) for arithmetic and exponential operations.
  • Press function buttons (sin, cos, tan, log, √) for scientific calculations.
  • Use parentheses for complex expressions.
  • Click "C" to clear the display.
  • Press "=" to compute the result.
  • Use "n!" for factorial calculations and "|x|" for absolute values.
