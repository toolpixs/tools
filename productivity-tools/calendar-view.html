Frequently Asked Questions

What views are available in the Calendar tool?

The tool offers month, week, and day views to help you plan at different levels of detail.

Is the Calendar View tool free?

Yes, it's completely free to use with no registration required.

Can I sync with Google or Outlook?

Currently, tasks are stored locally in your browser. Full Google/Outlook sync requires a backend, but you can import tasks from other ToolPix tools.

How are tasks saved?

Tasks are saved in your browser's local storage and persist until you clear them.
