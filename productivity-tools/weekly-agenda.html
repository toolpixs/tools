About Weekly Agenda Builder

ToolPix's Weekly Agenda Builder helps you plan your week with precision. Set clear goals, estimate time for each task, and assign them to specific days to stay organized. Key features include:

  • Time Estimates: Input estimated hours to manage your schedule effectively.
  • Priority Settings: Categorize tasks as low, medium, or high priority.
  • Easy Management: Edit or delete agenda items with a single click.
