        Frequently Asked Questions

        What is a Kanban Board?

        A Kanban Board is a visual tool for managing tasks, typically organized into columns like To Do, In Progress, and Done, to track workflow.

        Is the Kanban Board free?

        Yes, ToolPix's Kanban Board is completely free with no registration required.

        Are my tasks saved?

        Yes, your tasks are saved in your browser's local storage and persist across sessions until cleared.

        Can I move tasks between columns?

        Yes, you can drag and drop tasks between columns or change their status using the input form.
