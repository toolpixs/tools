    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is a gratitude journal?

    A gratitude journal is a tool for writing daily notes about what you're thankful for, helping to foster positivity and improve your mood.

    Is the Gratitude Journal free?

    Yes, ToolPix's Gratitude Journal is completely free with no registration required.

    Are my entries saved?

    Yes, your gratitude entries are saved in your browser's local storage and persist across sessions until cleared.

    Can I view entries from specific dates?

    Yes, use the date filter to view entries for a specific day or reset to see all entries.
