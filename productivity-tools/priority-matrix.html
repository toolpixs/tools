          Frequently Asked Questions

          What is the Priority Matrix?

          The Priority Matrix, also known as the Eisenhower Matrix, is a 2x2 grid that helps you categorize tasks based on urgency and importance to prioritize effectively.

          Is the Priority Matrix free?

          Yes, ToolPix's Priority Matrix is completely free with no registration required.

          Are my tasks saved?

          Yes, your tasks are saved in your browser's local storage and persist across sessions until cleared.

          Can I move tasks between quadrants?

          Yes, you can drag and drop tasks between quadrants or change their category using the input form.
