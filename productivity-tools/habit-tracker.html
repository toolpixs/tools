    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is a habit tracker?

    A habit tracker helps you monitor and maintain daily, weekly, or monthly habits, tracking streaks and progress to build consistency.

    Is the Habit Tracker tool free?

    Yes, ToolPix's Habit Tracker is completely free with no registration required.

    How are streaks calculated?

    Streaks are calculated based on consecutive completions of a habit according to its frequency (daily, weekly, or monthly).

    Are habits saved automatically?

    Yes, habits and their progress are saved in your browser's local storage and persist across sessions until cleared.
