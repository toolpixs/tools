    Frequently Asked Questions

    Why is this a simulation?

    Blocking URLs in a browser requires special permissions only available to browser extensions. This tool simulates blocking by disabling input and showing a warning.

    How can I actually block URLs?

    For real URL blocking, use browser extensions like StayFocusd (Chrome) or LeechBlock (Firefox), which have the necessary permissions.

    Is the Distraction Blocker free?

    Yes, ToolPix's Distraction Blocker is completely free with no registration required.

    Are blocked URLs saved?

    Yes, blocked URLs are saved in your browser's local storage and persist across sessions until cleared.
