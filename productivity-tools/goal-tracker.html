    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the Goal Tracker?

    The Goal Tracker helps you define goals, set milestones, and link tasks to track progress toward your objectives.

    Is the Goal Tracker free?

    Yes, ToolPix's Goal Tracker is completely free with no registration required.

    Are my goals saved?

    Yes, your goals, milestones, and tasks are saved in your browser's local storage and persist across sessions until cleared.

    Can I edit or delete goals?

    Yes, you can delete goals, milestones, and tasks. Editing is not supported directly, but you can delete and recreate items as needed.
