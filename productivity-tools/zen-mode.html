Frequently Asked Questions

What is Zen Mode?

Zen Mode provides a full-screen, distraction-free interface with a timer, notes, and motivational quotes to help you focus.

Is Zen Mode free?

Yes, ToolPix's Zen Mode is completely free with no registration required.

Can I save my notes?

Yes, notes are saved in your browser's local storage and persist across sessions until cleared.

How do I exit Zen Mode?

Click the "Exit" button or press the ESC key to return to the main page. Note that full-screen mode may require pressing F11 or your browser's full-screen toggle key to exit.
