Frequently Asked Questions

What is a daily planner, and why use one?

A daily planner helps you schedule tasks by time, prioritize work, and stay organized. It reduces stress and boosts productivity by keeping your day structured.

Is ToolPix's Daily Planner free?

Yes, our Daily Planner is completely free with no sign-up required. Access it anytime to plan your day.

Can I sync my tasks with Google Calendar or Outlook?

Sync functionality is planned but not yet available. Currently, tasks are saved locally in your browser.

How are my tasks saved?

Tasks are stored in your browser's local storage, ensuring they persist across sessions. Use the "Save Plan" button to update your saved plan.

Can I reorder tasks?

Yes, you can drag and drop time blocks to reorder tasks, making it easy to adjust your schedule.
