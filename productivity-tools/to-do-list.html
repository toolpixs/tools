    Frequently Asked Questions

    Why use a to-do list for task management?

    A to-do list helps you prioritize tasks, stay organized, and reduce stress by providing a clear overview of your responsibilities. It ensures you focus on what matters most.

    Is ToolPix's to-do list tool really free?

    Absolutely, our to-do list tool is free with no registration required. Use it anytime to manage your tasks efficiently.

    Can I set due dates for tasks?

    Yes, you can add due dates to each task using the date input field. Tasks are displayed with their due dates for better planning.

    How are tasks saved?

    Tasks are stored in your browser's local storage, so they remain available even after you close the page. Use the "Clear All" button to remove them when needed.

    Can I use this tool offline?

    Once loaded, the tool works offline as tasks are saved locally in your browser. However, an internet connection is needed to access other linked tools.
