Frequently Asked Questions

How can I report a problem with a tool? +

If you encounter any issues with our tools, please use our Bug Report form or email us at support@toolpix.com with details about the problem, including:

  • The tool you were using
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots if possible
  • Your browser and operating system
Do you offer premium or paid tools? +

Currently, all tools on ToolPix are completely free to use. We may introduce premium features in the future, but we'll always maintain a robust set of free tools for our users.

Can I suggest a new tool or feature? +

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for new tools or improvements to existing ones. Please use our Feature Request form to share your ideas with us.

How can I partner with ToolPix? +

We're open to partnerships with other companies and developers. Please email us at partnerships@toolpix.com with details about your proposal and how you'd like to collaborate.
